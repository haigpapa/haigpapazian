Sometimes I Wake Up Elsewhere

An autofiction dream cycle — 62 entries, ~28,000 words. No named sections; sequencing does the structural work. Surrealism arrives through physical detail. Beau and Griffin anchor the prose throughout. The ending stops in ordinary presence — it does not resolve.
