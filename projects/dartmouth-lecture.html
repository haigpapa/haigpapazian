Dartmouth College — Lecture

Year 2021
Status Complete
Role Lecturer

Invited lecture at Dartmouth College on multidisciplinary practice, exile architecture, and the cultural politics of sound. The lecture explored how the experience of building a band in Beirut — and then having it systematically banned — produces specific forms of architectural and systemic thinking about how cultural space is made and unmade.
