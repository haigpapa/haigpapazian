Current Research

The Localization Gap

All works →

AI music systems — Suno, Udio, MusicLM — default to Western harmonic frameworks. Arabic maqam systems, microtonal structures, and non-Western resolution logic are systematically erased. Not through malice. Through dataset monoculture.

427+ tracks generated and annotated via Python API pipelines. The question isn't whether the models can generate Arabic music — it's whether they can generate the actual thing, or only its Western approximation.

This is the intellectual spine of Walaw Studio and the reason the portfolio exists as a spatial system rather than a list of deliverables.
